2013 Tieni Duro Graduates are off to college! They can be spotted atUC Berkeley, UC Santa Barbara, St. Mary's, and DeAnza College.
The team will miss you all!

The grassroots division of Tieni Duro is proud to have signed up 9 new riders for late 2013/2014, with 5 of them being in the 15-16 age group!

Tieni Duro visits Nevada for the Little City Stage Race (Omnium) and takes 1st in GC in the 10-14s, and a stage victory as well as 4th and 5th in GC in the 17-18s.

Tieni Duro turns 14 in 2014. The team is now older than some of its members! A huge thanks to all of our sponsors below for your continued support.
